Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 13, 2026

The short version: RetireRichCalc does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. All calculator inputs live only in your browser and disappear when you close the page.

1. Who We Are

RetireRichCalc ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website retirerichcalc.com, a free financial calculator tool. This privacy policy explains what data is collected when you use the site and how it is used.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your calculator inputs (savings amounts, ages, rates, targets)
  • Account details or financial information
  • IP addresses or device fingerprints linked to your identity

There is no sign-up, no account system, and no backend database. Every number you enter stays in your browser tab.

3. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to RetireRichCalc and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data when you visit sites that use its advertising services, see: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics tools to understand aggregate site traffic — such as which calculators are most used and how visitors navigate the site. Any analytics data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users.

5. Links to External Sites

RetireRichCalc may contain links to third-party websites. We have no control over the content or privacy practices of those sites and are not responsible for their policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

6. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through this site, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at: privacy@retirerichcalc.com
